Popular Places

Kanyakumari,Tamilnadu

Kanyakumari is the Southernmost city of India.The Indian Ocean,Arabian Sea,Bay of Bengal are joining at Kanyakumari. Kanyakumari is famous for Sunrise and Sunset over the oceans and home to BhagavathiAmman temple.The Tiruvalluvar Statue and Vivekananda Statue are present in center of the oceans.

Alleppey,Kerala

Allepey is home to Houseboats and one of the popular and most visited tourist spot in India.Alleppey is present along the malabar coast sorrounded by Arabian sea and Kumarakom Wetlands.Allepey has beautiful beaches,Islands,Wetlands and Vembanad Lake and one of top tourist destination of India.

Goa,India

Goa has many tourist attractions.It is Sorrounded by Western Ghats and Beautiful Arabian Sea in the Konkan coast of India.Goa is a home to beaches,forts and falls.Dudhsagar Falls is located in goa and third largest waterfall in India.The Baga Beach and Calangute Beach are the most famous beaches in Goa.
